The Philippines is an island country with more than 7,600 islands located in southeastern Asia, and known for it's scenic mountains, enchanting beaches, exotic wildlife, majestic volcanoes, friendly people, rich culture and tradition, exciting nightlife and delicious cuisines. If any of these things sound interesting to you, this might just the perfect place for YOUR, next vacation!

But before then, you might want to know some basic info that will be helpfull when starting the trip to the Philippines. Tips

One should always know some basics about where one's going when traveling anywhere, and the Philippines is no exception. Here are 8 basic points:

  1. Manila is the capital city of the Philippines, located in Luzon, the largest island region.
  2. Luzon, together with Visayas and Mindanao, are the three main Filipino island regions.
  3. Even though English can easily be used in the Philippines, most people would probably speak in their own tongue, Filipino, the most used dialekt being Tagalog.
  4. The currency used there is the Filipino Peso (The safest option is searching Google).
  5. With a mix of a hot and humid climate, you should expect to sweat, so better pack light and airy clothes! One nice thing you can do is putt a towel on your back in your t-shirt, this will not only collect some of the sweat, but you can also save space for other things while drying yourself! Thanks mom!
  6. Mosquitos and other bugs and creepy crawlys lurk around every corner, just so you know ;)
  7. The sun is mighty and powerful, so sunscreen is recommended!
  8. Taking both points above to account, bug and sunscreen are readily available in the Philippines for purchasing if you forget to bring your own.

Now that you know some handy tips after having taken a look at everything offered here you are probably feeling ready to book a flight! Sadly, you're on your own on this one, I'm only here to give some tips, not book flights for you :'(

Good luck on your next journey!
